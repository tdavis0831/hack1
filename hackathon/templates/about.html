What is Restorative Justice?

Restorative Justice is a theory that fcouses on rehabilitation and mending instead of punishing people. Research shows that BIPOC are more likely to recieve a minimum sentence for drug crimes than white people. Approximately 80 percent of people are in prision for drug related crimes and of that, 60 percent are BIPOC. It is clear that drug laws are unjust and have been used to target BIPOC. This has been going on since the 1980s with the "war on drugs" that has done nothing but stigmatized drug use, destroyed communities, and broken families apart. Now that many states are moving to decriminalize certain drug offenses and it is vital that we demand justice.
