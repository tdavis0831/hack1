Brands that Support Restorative Justice

Last Prisioner Project Project founded in 2019 dedicated to "freeing every last prisoner of the unjust war on drugs, starting with the estimated 40,000 individuals imprisioned for cannabis"

The Floret Coalition "An anti-ractist collective of small businesses in the cannabis and cannabis-adjacent space supporting and funding equity-oriented actions via monthly donations and social campaigns"

Lady Jays A cannabis line out of Portland that donates all proceeds from various pre-roll packs to the Last Prisioner Project

Leune A California based women of color owned business that created Leune Lab to showcase creative work that has donated all proceeds from face maks to the Last Prisioner Project

Green Thumb Industries A cannabis brand committed "to a future where those who have suffered criminally as a result of cannabis and systemic racism are not only expunged of those ‘crimes’ but also welcomed into the cannabis community."

Leafly A site dedicated to aide in the discovery of cannabis products. Lafly is "on a mission to strengthen our industry through equity, and unlock economic empowerment for those trapped by outdated policy. We will lead with, and be led by, justice for all.
